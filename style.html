Styling Tips from Pros

  • Face shape matters: Match your hairstyle with your face shape for best results.
  • Product selection: Use a matte finish product for a natural look, or high-shine for formal occasions.
  • Hydration is key: Drink water and moisturize daily for healthy hair and skin.
  • Regular trims: Maintain your style with haircuts every 4-6 weeks.
  • Consult your barber: They can recommend styles that work with your hair type and lifestyle.
